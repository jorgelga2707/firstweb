We will find your Buddy, and bring it home!

We are a non-profit organization located in Lima Peru that was born from the need to help people who have lost a pet friend, we want cover all the country.

Our mission is to help you find your pet with the help oour web platform and to have more happy homes with your beloved pets.

We have collaborators all over the country, who help us post the images of the pets. Check in the facebook and instagram groups, repost the last locations seen to be able to establish a search range.

Using a sofware that connect to the database of all the social network, and our users can post the last place where your pet was seen.

Because we know all the homes need have their buddy's with them, we start the entrepreneurship in facebook and instagram web pages.

Now with the help of investors and a group of engineers, we have develop the software that will help you fin your lost dog.
